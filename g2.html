Background

Jason is a year four computer science undergraduate in NUS. Since his study focus lies into a computer-related area, he interacts with computers every and is very familiar with the computer systems. He has been in NUS for seven semesters and has taken over thirty modules. Among those modules, over 70% requires one or more textbooks for the students to follow. Jason thus has accumulated a large bunch of textbooks of the modules he has taken. Hence, he decides to sell those used-textbooks instead throwing into into trash bins directly. He tried a few online used textbook trading platforms, but very few juniors contact him and ask about the textbooks.


Goals
  • Primary Goal
    • Selling certain used textbooks
  • Sub-Goals
    • Post Textbook Information:
      “I would like to post textbook information and my contact information on the platform in order to advertising my book. These info may include the book title, the module it is used in, a picture of the cover, its author, version, condition and my phone numbers.”

    • View the popularity of a certain textbook:
      “It would be great if I can see the need of the books I want to sell in the market and check if it has already been in someone’s wishing list. It’s even better if I can know the wisher’s name and contact info, since I would contact them directly rather than wait for some unknown buyers to contact me.”

    • View the average price of a certain textbook:
      “I don’t really know how to price my book. If I can see the average price of the existing ones, maybe it would be easier for me to set my price.”

    • Share the post:
      “Sometimes I want to post the selling info to my social networks, but it’s really boring and troublesome to enter all those information again. It would be much more convenient if there is a button for me to share the post directly.”

Background

Lucy has just joined NUS and she doesn’t know many seniors in the university. She is majored in Chinese Study in Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences and thus she is not very familiar with the computer systems. In the first lectures of the semester, she was told that she would need to buy the textbooks used in the module to catch up with the lecturer’s teaching progress. However, when she walked into bookstores and finally find the right book, she was shocked by the high price. As a result, she turns to seek a platform to buy the used-textbooks in good condition with much lower price. She has tried the textbook forum provided by the school. However, when she logged into the page, she was confused and didn’t know where to start. After several tries, she found the textbook which she wanted. However, after she left a message under the post, she never got replies. She guessed that the seller might have already sold the book or he/she never check the post again.


Goals
  • Primary Goal
    • Buy certain used-textbooks
  • Sub-Goals
    • Search for the book:
      “The most important thing for me is to find my desired book. Sometimes I may need to search for the module code of the corresponding module, or I may need to directly search the book’s name.”
    • Compare the price:
      “After all, the reason why I chose to buy a used-textbook is its low price. It would be great if the qualified books (after searching) can be sorted with respect to their price. Then It would be easier for me to choose only from the cheapest ones.”
    • Compare the condition:
      “Some books with super low price are in very bad conditions and this is not the kind I want. I want to be able to compare the condition of the books and choose the one with best condition and reasonable price.”
    • Contact with seller effectively:
      “I could not get the response from the seller in time. I would like to know whether the item has been sold and I would like to contact the seller in a more effecient way. ”
