• UI problems can be generally divided into three categories.
  • Redundant functions and icons which makes the interface become very messy.
  • The interface does not provide sufficient information.
  • The whole interface is not attractive and colorful.
  • Templates for sellers are needed to help them post quickly without forgetting to provide some useful information.
  • Condition levels of the textbook are not clearly stated.
  • Page loading time is too long.
  • Feedback time of seller or buyer needs to be improved.
  • We may leave audio messages out of the first several iterations of design, but we need a simple way for the users to upload their pictures easily.
  • The uploading process should be directly access with mobile devices to avoid the troubles brought by the inter-devices transfer process.
  • We need a picture-friendly layout to support easy browsing of all the pictures uploaded.
  • Searching is indeed the soul of a used textbook forum, however current system is not perfect yet.
  • Users prefer a fast search that can immediately give them results.
